Strong partners and innovative ideas for a sustainable future

Create a team and develop solutions for climate challenges put forward by the City of Graz, Land Steiermark, Holding Graz and Energie Graz about the future of micro climate, mobility, public transportation and photovoltaics

The Climathon in Graz will give you the opportunity to get involved and be a part of how your city changes in the future. Turn your vision into a solution by working with experts in your field of interest and find new ways of thinking. You can come with a preexisting idea or simply be part of a team that will have the chance to win prizes and further develop your idea after the climathon. There will be experts, mentors, drinks and food to support you through this 24 hour hackathon!

Find solutions to our Challenges for a sustainable future

Together with our challenge partners we have identified four fields of interest - public transportation, photovoltaic, mobility and micro climate - support us with your brainpower!

How can citizens contribute to social transformation for a climate friendly transport system?

Social changes are needed in order to stop climate change. Which social changes of the coming decades are relevant to the evolution of mobility? How can our transport system look like in 2050 in regards to climate protection?

Read more

How can efficient usage of renewable energy be ensured to reduce losses and the need for storage?

The usage and production of renewable energy should be better linked in order to be more efficient and strengthen the production of renewable energy in cities. How can residents be motivated to be more aware of the advantages of their home-generated renewable energy from the roof?

Read more

What will an interlinked public transportation network look like in the future?

Public Transport becomes more and more important for cities. How could public transport look like in the future? Are there new ways of public transportation to be found? How can residents be motivated to use public transport on a daily basis?

Read more

How can climate and air quality data be made more understandable for citizens?

Understanding micro climate can help being more climate friendly. But how can this data be used to motivate citizens to be aware of their behaviour in their daily life routine?

Schedule for the day

Check out our schedule for the 24-hours starting on friday the 19th October

14:30 - 15:00

Registration

Participants are registering themselves

15:00 - 16:00

Welcome

Opening speech and presentation of the challenges

16:00 - 16:45

Start ot the 24-hour Climathon and Teamregistration

Teams are registering and will choose their challenge

18:30 - 19:00

Pep Talk

Johanna Pirker will talk about ways to get and stay motivated

19:00 - 20:00

Dinner

Regional and ecological food will be served

20:00 - 21:00

Workshop: Business Model Canvas

Key questions that need to be answered to create a successful business model will be discussed. Participants will work in groups and will be guided by a mentor.

23:20 - 00:00

Energizing Exercise

Participants will have the chance to get energy again

00:00 - 00:30

Midnightsnack

Regional and ecological food will be served

06:30 - 07:00

Good Morning Exercise

Participants will have the chance to get energy again

07:00 - 08:00

Breakfast

Regional and ecological food will be served

08:00 - 09:00

Workshop: Financial Planning and Funding

Participants will learn about financial planning. They will be provided with an overview of different funding options for environmentally friendly business startups.

11:00 - 12:00

Pitch-Workshop

Participants will learn to deliver an engaging and informative pitch when presenting their idea. The focus will be on structure, the WOW-moment and confidence when pitching.

12:00 - 13:00

Lunch

Regional and ecological food will be served

15:00

Deadline of working on the challenges

Teams will have to submit their solution

15:15 - 16:00

Presentation of the Solutions

Teams will present their solutions to the judges

16:15 - 16:45

Ceremony

Winners will be announced

16:45 - 18:00

Afterparty

Relaxation and Connection Time

Creating Innovative Solutions for London's Environmental Challenges

How can London eliminate single use plastic waste and transition from Waste Management Systems to Resource Management Systems?

With space limitations, the high cost of depositing waste to landfill and increased awareness on the impact of current waste management systems on the natural environment, avoiding “waste” generation has continued to increase in importance.

Improved resource management practices which embrace Sustainability and Circular Economy principles can provide opportunities to improve the overall resource efficiency of urban areas, create new jobs and stimulate economic growth in new areas, helping to address the environmental impact of current consumption and waste disposal practices.

A major challenge for cites and the wider environment is the accumulation of plastic waste, especially single use plastic waste which is inherently difficult to manage in a sustainable manner due to the composite nature of the material finished product and high energy inputs required during manufacturing.

Be part of the Solution.

Work with like minded peopel in partnership with  business and industry experts to support the creation of viable solutions to our resource use and plastic waste challenges.

We will sponsor the most innovative,  practical and ambitious ideas to help make them a reality. 

We will provide a stimulating environment that will support creative and innovative thinking and problem solving, and a platform for collaborative working and creation with a diverse set of stakeholders form industry experts to businesses, academics and community leaders.

Climathon London 2018 does not end on October 26th. It's just the beginning as the real work begins following this creative partnership event which will lead to new connections and collaborative partnerships and with a bit of luck some genuine advances in creating viable sustainable environmental solutions.

We want London to have an advanced and world leading resource management system, where jobs are created, and businesses and economies are developed to implement intelligent and efficient use of resources.

We want to see creative connections and lines of communication between designers, manufacturer's, supppliers, and users which leads to the elimination of waste and the circualtion of high value resouces within the London economy.

London is a great city and we want to see this reflected not just in the economy, fantastic architucture and restraunts, but also in the natural environment that supports our lifestyles health and wellbeing. 

Intelligent and considerate use of resources applying Circular Economy principles will mean that London can not only benefit from a healthier environment locally, but contribute to reduced pollution and environmental impact on a global scale.

Our vision is one where products are designed and manufactured intelligently, with the importance and value of the natural environmet and resources are valued and prioritisd.

Our vision is one where resources are highly valued, the impact of their extraction minimised and the harmful impact of waste on oceans sea shores and the landscape is eliminated.

Whether you are refining your idea or you're ready to grow, Echo++ will tailor a support package to your needs and connect you with local experts who can help set your business on the path to success.

 

Advance London

The Advance London programme is a holistic programme to enable SMEs in London to capitalise on the benefits of the circular economy.  The programme includes a free business support service that is jointly funded by LWARB and the European Regional Development Fund for SMEs looking to scale their circular economy business or those looking to transition to a more circular business model.

All support is bespoke to meet your particuar business or innovation needs.

The winning team will receive further support from Advance London, provided they are formally incorporated. They will be part of Advance London programme and will be invited to a series of workshops to launch and scale up their business.

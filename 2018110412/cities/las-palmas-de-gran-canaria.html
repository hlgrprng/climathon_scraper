Together we can make Gran Canaria and the world a better place to live! 

Solve the climate challenge put forward by our cities about the future of zero emissions. 

Join the Climathon in Gran Canaria to connect with city officials and partners to bring about a shared vision for a healthier cities. Turn this vision into a reality by working together with developers, entrepreneurs, or students to find innovative city solutions and win support. Come with an existing idea or simply work on a new solution with an inspiring team. Experts will support your team during the 24 hours in finding the best solutions to the challenges.

Prizes

Win support for your Climathon solution.

FIRST PRIZE: € 1000 + Advice from the SPEGC Business Support Service to undertake innovation projects. Advice from the Industrial and Intellectual Property Office (OPII) of the University of Las Palmas of Gran Canaria, for industrial protection of the proposed solution. It will be proposed by the Telefónica Chair ULPGC to be part of open innovation programs of Telefónica, such as Open Future, or Think Big of Fundación Telefónica. Single annual bonus for «syticleta» local service for all team members (maximum of 5).

SECOND PRIZE: € 600 + Advice from the SPEGC Business Support Service to undertake innovation projects. It will be proposed by the Telefónica Chair ULPGC to be part of open innovation programs of Telefónica, such as Open Future, or Think Big of Fundación Telefónica. Single annual bonus for «syticleta» local service for all team members (maximum of 5).

THIRD PRIZE: € 400 + Advice from the SPEGC Business Support Service to undertake innovation projects. It will be proposed by the Telefónica Chair ULPGC to be part of open innovation programs of Telefónica, such as Open Future, or Think Big of Fundación Telefónica.

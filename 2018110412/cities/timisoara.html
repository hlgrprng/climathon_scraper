Join us in creating innovative smart responses to climate change effects!

Timisoara Climathon brings together individuals and organizations in a joint effort to shape city's response to the effects of climate change!

Join the Climathon in Timișoara to connect with people with different backgrounds belonging to various public and private organizations and groups, all concerned about the future of our urban environment. Together we'll generate innovative ideas and solutions that will make our city a more sustainable and climate change resilient place.

You may come with an existing idea or simply contribute to definition of a newly generated solution with an inspiring team.

Experts and mentors will introduce relevant concepts, present globally relevant stories and will support teams' work during the intensive 24 hour event.

We can jointly contribute to equip Timisoara, one of the 2021 European Capitals of Culture with local- and visitor-centric innovative solutions!

Get ready, join the climathon and put your fingerprint on a better future in our Timisoara!

Together we can make Trentino and the world a better place to live!

Help us to solve the Rovereto and province of Trento challenges for a more universal and sustainable mobility!

Global hackathon

Climathon is a global movement dedicated to solving the toughest climate challenges cities face today. In October 2018, in its fourth year of operation, the 24-hour climate hackathon takes place in more than 124 cities on 6 continents and comes to Trentino for the second time!

Climathon in Rovereto

Join the Climathon @Rovereto, the city of Peace and Innovation. Connect with public officials and partners to bring your ideas for a smarter region. Turn this vision into a reality by working together with developers, entrepreneurs, researchers, experts, citizens or students to find innovative solutions for the mobility of the city of Rovereto and the Province of Trentino. Come with an existing idea or simply work on a new solution with an inspiring team. Experts and mentors will support your team during the 24 hours in finding the best solutions to the challenges.

Challenges

Provincia autonoma di Trento and Comune di Rovereto launched four challenges concerning urban and extraurban sustainable and inclusive mobility: these exciting challenges are waiting to be solved by you.

How can we support road sharing by motor vehicles, bicycles and pedestrians in Rovereto city?

Rovereto administration wants to promote cycling and slow mobility in general, including the theme of “pedestrianlity” and architectural barriers. Not only in terms of more bike and pedestrian routes, but also with a series of measures fostering more trips by bike and on foot.

Read more

How can we promote the use of public transport in Vallagarina?

Strengthening of Public transport is a strategic axis of the new Urban Mobility Plan of Rovereto (“PUM”). Relevant investments have been planned starting from 2018. In which way can the administration address and support strengthened public transport offering to increase public transport users?

Read more

How can we share with citizens a process of limiting cars in the center of Rovereto?

The centre of Rovereto is an attractive place. The pressure of traffic on the incoming roads and on parking lots is strong. Reduce this pressure without loosing attractiveness particularly pertains to the economic activities that operate there, but also the residents, the workers, the "City users".

Read more

How can we implement efficient, effective and universal modal transport in the region of Trentino?

Sustainable mobility in the Alpine regions, particularly those close the Brenner corridor, is affected by many challenges and constrains. Future transit of millions of people and increased movement needs of residents into the valleys, will imply new plans of integrated modal transport.

What's in it for me?

  • Me
  • My city
  • The world
  • Join the event and get the chance to solve one of the exciting challenges offered by the City of Rovereto and the Province of Trento: universal mobility, Rovereto future mobility.

  • Enjoying a more prosperous, climate-resilient future
  • Engaging local entrepreneurs, students, professionals, developers and innovators, citizens on climate action

Prizes

Win a reward for your solution

The winner team will be rewarded with:

  • free participation of each participant to the entrepreneurial training academy ("Innovation Academy 2019") organised by Trentino Sviluppo;
  • cash prize € 2.500,00 granted in equally subdivided amounts to every team member.

The prize is to be understood as comprehensive of administrative and tax burdens to the possible extent which they are due. In the event that the Evaluation Committee does not identify a winner, the aforementioned awards will not be granted.

Moreover, the winner team will participate in the Climathon event organized by Climate-KIC Italy which will be held on 8th November in Rimini at Ecomondo, the leading fair of the green and circular economy in the Euro-Mediterranean area. The fair combines in a single platform all sectors of the circular economy: from material recovery and energy to sustainable development.

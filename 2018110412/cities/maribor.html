Comprehend and Copy Nature - solutions for a regenerative Maribor

City of Maribor is the second largest city in Slovenia and it is the leading gravity centre of Podravje region and Eastern cohesion region of Slovenia. City of Maribor is signatory of Covenant of Mayors for Climate & Energy, title holder of „Alpine town of the year“ with officially adopted "Strategy for the transition to circular economy". Maribor urban functional area has ca.105.000 inhabitants with the 2nd largest University in Slovenia. Maribor geographical position is on the juncture of roads connecting Central Europe with Southern Europe and Western Europe with the Pannonian plain. Due to its distance of just 18 km from the Austrian border, Maribor represents the gateway into the country as well as to the Balkans. Maribor is developing itself from a once post-2nd world-war, industrial city into a dynamic, green, touristic-cultural and economic center of the northeastern Slovenia. Municipality of Maribor is responsible for local matters of public interest.

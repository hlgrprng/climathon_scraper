How can we move in Ferrara in a more sustainable way?

Ferrara is recognized worldwide as "city of bikes", but many people in Ferrara use cars for commuting, shopping, leisure activities, picking children up from school, etc. How can we innovate mobility in Ferrara?

Join the Climathon in Ferrara to connect with city officials, private companies, university and schools to bring your solution about real sustainable mobility in Ferrara. Turn your vision and ideas into reality by working together with city officials, makers, private companies, teachers, children and parents to find innovative city solutions. Come with an existing idea or simply work on a new solution with an inspiring team. Experts and mentors will support your team during the Climathon in finding the best solutions to the challenges.

Why should I take a bike instead of using my car?

According to a recent survey by the Municipality of Ferrara, almost 50% of citizens use private cars or motorbikes for their home-work trips and for shopping. This percentage is still valid also when considering only residents that live and work inside Ferrara.

Read more

How long, in feet, is the path from home to school?

In Ferrara, parents as well as the Municipality ask for the reduction of cars in front of schools; focus is not only on traffic jams during peak-times, but on improving health and safety of children, supporting families in their daily need to pick students to school.

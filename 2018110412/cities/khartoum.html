Join Climathon Khartoum 2018 and help in solving the Climate challenges facing the city.

Join Climathon Khartoum 2018 to connect with different Climate Change actors to bring about a shared vision for a better Khartoum. Turn this vision into a reality by working together with software developers, entrepreneurs, students and other citizens interested in Climate Action to find innovative solutions and win support. Frequently asked questions

What is Climathon Khartoum?

The Climathon is a 24-hour hackathon, dedicated to solving the extreme weather challenges facing Khartoum. It will take place simultaneously in 100+ cities around the world on 26 October 2018.

Why should I participate?

Climathon offers you the chance to take action to create real solutions to Khartoum's climate challenges. You can also benefit from:

• Helping to make your city more resilient to climate change
• Networking with local leaders from academia, business, and public authorities
• Developing your skills in public speaking, innovative thinking, prioritisation, and exploring new tools and methodologies
• Joining forces with other like-minded entrepreneurs, students and developers
• Generating ideas and solutions to climate change that can become real-life businesses
• Becoming a part of a global community working together to take climate action!

Who is eligible to apply?

  • Entrepreneurs
  • Students
  • Software Developers
  • Innovators
  • Policy Makers
  • Professionals
  • Other citizens interested in climate action.

What is the application process?

  • You must first fill out and submit your application through Khartoum’s city page, before the appropriate deadlines.
  • If accepted, our team will notify you and further update you about the Pre-ideation workshop and the Climathon event.

Dates and times of the Climathon?

The Climathon will take place on the 26-27th  October 2018, starting at 14:30 pm.

What is the application deadline?

10 October 2018

What to expect from Climathon?

  1. Explore challenges and form teams
    Participants will find more about the city’s climate challenge and form teams
  2. Find solutions and refine them
    Teams will ideate to come up with solutions and refine these solutions further using different methodologies and discussing it with experts.
  3. Present in front of a jury & win support
    Teams pitch their solutions to a jury, who will choose best ideas and announce the winning teams.

What do I bring?

Personal items: Special medications, Toothbrushes, toothpastes, sleeping aids, headphones, labtop, and chargers.

What if I don’t know much about Climate change?

You are still welcome to apply! Bring your expertise. As long as you come willing to learn and ready to contribute ideas, you are welcome to apply.

I can’t stay the whole 24 hours; can I still participate?

No, unfortunately! You have to commit to stay for 24-hours.

Are there any registration fees?

No, there are no registration fees.

You did not find the answer? Please contact one of the organisers

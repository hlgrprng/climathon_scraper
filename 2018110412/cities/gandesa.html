Together we can make The Terra Alta a better place to live and make a global impact!

Climathon is a global movement dedicated to solving the toughest climate challenges that we face today. In October 2018, in its fourth year of operation, the 24-hour climate Hackathon takes place in more than 100 cities. We will work to improve the resilience of the rural areas to climate change

Join the Climathon in Gandesa to connect with fellows to bring a shared vision for a healthier region. Turn this vision into a reality by working together with developers, engineers, activists, marketers, entrepreneurs, or students.

Find solution for current problems about water, forest, energy and mobility. For the main climate concerns for rural areas. It doesn't mater if you want to make an awareness campaign, an NGO, a business or a service learning solution.

Come with an existing idea or simply work on a new solution with an inspiring team. Experts and coaches will be on site to help you.

How to get there

By train: R15 and R16 from Barcelona and tarragona to Mora la Nova or Tortosa, where to take the train.

By bus: Direct buses from bus stations of Mora la Nova and Tortosa. From Tarragona you can take a Monbus.

On foot/bike: It's in the industrial park of gandesa, 4 minutes away by food from Gandesa city center.

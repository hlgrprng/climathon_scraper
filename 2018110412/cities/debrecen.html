Together we can make Debrecen and the world a better place to live!

This event will be Hungarian only! Solve various climate-related challenges provided by municipalities of Debrecen as well as our partners

Debrecen is the "Capital city of the Great Hungarian Plain", and the second largest city in the country with about 200,000 inhabitants and historically the most important city in Hungarian protestantism. It used to be Europe's largest Calvinist city (was called "The Calvinist Rome"), and the Grand Church (Nagytemplom) is a reminder of the city's heritage.

Nowadays it is the City of Endless Possibilities, including a flourishing university with 35,000 students (among them 5,500+ foreign students); a bustling art life has made Debrecen the cultural hub of the region; the city has an extensive healthcare network, and has unlimited potential around Debrecen.

How can we find innovative solutions for traffic reduction, dust prevention and to raise awareness?

One of the biggest climate problem in Debrecen is the nationally high level of Air Pollution. This is caused by the always growing number of traffic, and the dust coming from rural areas into the city. With the help of the citizens we can change the situation and raise awareness around these issues.

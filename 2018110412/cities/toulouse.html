Together we can make Toulouse and the world a better place to live!

Come and solve the climate challenge faced by the cities of Toulouse Metropole with Copernicus space data and tools! Join us for a 24 hours-experiment of pure creativity inspired by our climate and space experts.

Aerospace Valley inaugurates the era of hackathons within the B612, the new Toulouse Metropolitan building dedicated to innovation in the space and aeronautics sector.

This international event is a clever mix between two events of the kind: 

  • Organized on 26th October and focusing on Climate change, this event is supported by Climate-KIC as part of the global event (104 cities, 45 countries) of the Climathon
  • Focusing on the use of satellite images to meet climate challenges, it is supported and labelled by the European Commission as part of the Copernicus Hackathon

The objective of this event is to foster the emergence of new ideas and the creation of start-ups. To win the competition, the teams will have to deliver innovative solutions with high growth potential in 24 hours, using satellite data to fight climate change.

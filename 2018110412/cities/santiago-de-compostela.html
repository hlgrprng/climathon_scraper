Together we can make Galicia and the world a better place to live!

Let's fight together against climate change by reducing waste

Climate change is the biggest challenge that Galicia and the world are facing today. Join the Climathon to share your vision about a low carbon Galicia, join others professionals, researchers, students, representatives of the administration and toguether we will look for solutions and identify innovative ideas to improve waste management in Galicia and thus reduce the greenhouse emissions associated with them.

Join Climathon in Santiago de Compostela

Join the Climathon and help Galicia to be a lowcarbon region

Don´t miss the opportunity to be a hero in the fight against climate change through innovative ideas that could help improve the waste managements and enhance the circular economy

How can we fight climate change managing our waste?

The 3 Rs from Reduce, Reuse and Recycle are not enought and Galician requires solutions to implement all the R of the Circular Economy: Rethink, Redesign, Refabricate, Repair, Redistribute, Reduce, Reuse, Recycle, Recover Energy ... each and every one of these R are the key for a zero waste region

Cagliari Sustainable City

Together we can make Cagliari a better place to live!

The Municipality of Cagliari and the Regional Department of Defense of the Enviroment, with the support of the University of Cagliari and the Network of technical professions, partecipates in Climathon 2018, the climate adaptation initiative promoted by Climate-KIC. It will be an opportunity to reflect and propose innovative visions on the most important themes of " sustainable city"  and adaptation to climate change, according to the challenges that Cagliari will face through its application to the prestigious European Green Capital Award for the edition 2021: land use, water managment, heat islands.

Climathon 2018 represents a significant step, an important opportunity to strengthen partnerships and cooperation on sustainability issues, creating new synergies between public administrations, citizens, associations and business for a shared vision on the future of the city.

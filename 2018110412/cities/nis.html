Let's make Niš a healthier and better place to live

Climathon will be held in Niš this year for the first time, organized by ENECA and Proaktiv Association, in Deli co-working space on October 26th. The Climathon participants will develop solutions for the problems that the city of Niš is facing in the field of climate change within 24 hours.

Niš is one of the oldest cities in the Balkans. It was founded in the third century BC by Celts. Niš is situated on the river Nišava, after which it was named and is the third largest city in Serbia. Niš is located at the crossroads of Balkan and European roads connecting Europe to the Middle East and has been in existence since ancient times as the gate of the East and the West. Also, its one of the most developed cities in Serbia and is an industrial and tourist center of national importance. Join the Climathon in Nis to connect with city officials and partners to bring about a shared vision for a healthier city. Turn this vision into a reality by working together with developers, entrepreneurs, students to find innovative city solutions and win support. Come with an existing idea or simply work on a new solution with an inspiring team. Experts and mentors will support your team during the 24 hours in finding the best solutions to the challenges.

If you take action as part of the global Climathon movement, you can accomplish a lot of benefits. If you take action as part of the global Climathon movement, you can accomplish a lot of benefits.

• Develop your skills in public speaking, innovative thinking, and explore new tools and methodologies. 

• Help solve your city’s local climate challenge, and make your city more resilient to climate change

• Meeting like-minded people and become part of the Climathon movement

• Network with local leaders from academia, business and public authorities 

• Turning a climate solution into a business—and get support       

• Join forces with other like-minded entrepreneurs and innovators

Like any big city, Nis faces a number of  climate challenges. By organizing hackathon some of these problems can be solved.

•Identify climate innovations and solutions which can improve public   health  through changing citizen behaviours

• Creating the conditions for a prosperous, climate-resilient future

• Engaging local entrepreneurs, students, developers and innovators on climate action

• Networking with new global partners through EIT Climate-KIC

Niš is the third largest city in Serbia and it has the potential to look at climate issues from multiple aspects. By engaging young, minded people through hakaton, it is possible to come up with various, brilliant ideas for solving these problems. The successful implementation of these solutions in Nis can also be applied to other cities in the world.

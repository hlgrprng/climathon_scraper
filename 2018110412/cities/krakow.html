Together we can make Kraków and the whole world a better place to live!

We invite you to participate in the 3rd edition of Climathon in Kraków - an event that will add you health! :)

Come and join the Climathon in Kraków. You'll have a unique opportunity to work on climate change related issues and their impact on Kraków.

Climate change is a big threat to your health and life. What negative effects are we seeing today?

  • Storms and intense rain with hail, which paralyze the functioning of cities
  • Rising costs of food, energy and renovation of houses
  • Increasingly difficult leisure conditions, including the so-called phenomenon of snow poverty
  • Extremely high temperatures and drought in summer, which hinder life in built-up city centres
  • More and more frequent: droughts, lack of water
  • Shortages of food in cities caused by problems in agriculture
  • The number of diseases increases due to new viruses
  • Increased allergy symptoms, due to higher spring temperatures

Frequently asked questions

What is Climathon?

This is an international climate protection event, which is a 24-hour meeting, during which participants in cooperation with invited experts develop solutions for climate protection.

Who can take part in Climathon?

Local, non-governmental, local and business organizations, politicians, committed residents, innovators, entrepreneurs, creators and enthusiasts, young professionals and students are invited to participate.

Do I have to be 18 years old to come to Climathon?

No. You can come if you are over 15, but with the parent's consent.

Is participation in Climathon payable?

Participation in Climathon is free. We provide catering during the event. The participants cover their travel expenses on their own.

Can I come on my own or do I have to work in groups?

Contest participants can prepare the project individually or in groups by creating a team of up to 5 people.

Are diplomas provided for participation in the project?

Yes, each participant and winner will get a certificate.

Are rewards provided for the winner?

Yes, the winning team will receive a financial prize in the amount of PLN 4,000, as well as other prizes listed in the Regulations of the Competition.

What should you take at Climathon?

The most important thing is to have a laptop with you because we will mainly work on it during the whole event.

You did not find the answer? Please contact one of the organisers

Together we can make Novi Sad and the Balkans a better place to live!

Mitigating climate change trough resource efficiency. Join us in reducing environmental footprint of Novi Sad.

Join the Climathon in Novi Sad, the smartest city in Serbia. Join the movement in the city that is European Youth Capital 2019 and European Capital of Culture 2021. Come and work with us on ideas worth sharing and help us make Novi Sad a smart city before we host hundreds of thousands of tourists in 2019 and 2021. Your voice counts, come and share your ideas and lets implement them together with our partners in Novi Sad. 

Join us in Novi Sad, one of the fastest growing cities in the Balkans, and support local efforts to promote sustainable urbanization. Students, young professionals, programmers, urban planners, policy makers, entrepreneurs, you are all welcome! Come with an existing idea or work on a new solution with a diverse team. Experts and moderators will be there to help you to develop your idea. 

Are you ready?

Truly, we can not wait to begin!

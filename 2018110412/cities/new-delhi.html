Hack to breathe - Together let's make Delhi a breathable city

Climathon Delhi is an ideation stage platform to crowd-source innovative solutions to make the city's air cleaner

Delhi's air is getting more polluted day by day. According to the WHO, the value of PM10 should be 60 to 100 annually. In November 2017, it reached 999 micrograms per cubic meter, and in the latest reports it was recorded at 292 of May 2018. The aim of this event is to bring researchers, professionals,entrepreneurs,students and coders together to develop solutions (in form of Product/ Web Solution/ Business Idea/ Policy Advocacy) to address the issue of increasing pollution.

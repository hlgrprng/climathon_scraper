Wasting our waste

Solve the Šabac's challenge and help us answer where to when the trash can is full as well

Šabac is a city on the Sava river bank famous for its nickname “Little Paris” - which it received because of an extremely accentuated bohemian life in the past. Historically, Šabac held an enormous strategic and historical relevance due to its position, constantly on the crossroads of Bosnia, Belgrade, Srem and Podrinje. Today, it is one hour away from two major cities of Serbia, Belgrade and Novi Sad, as well as the border with Bosnia. Šabac has a dynamic and growing economy, especially in the trading industry.

Šabac is a developing city with a territory of 795km² and approximately 130.000 inhabitants. One of the biggest problems Šabac is faced with is waste management.

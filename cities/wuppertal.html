Together for a climate-friendly Wuppertal!

Become part of the Climathon Wuppertal and work together with a team on innovative solutions for climate challenges. Wuppertal is on its way to foster sustainable packaging, to prepare for future floodings and heavy rain events as well as to develop sustainable neighbourhood concepts.

Be part of the first Climathon in Wuppertal, exchange views with up to 80 like-minded people and contribute to the vision of climate-friendly urban development. Together with an inspiring team of start-ups, companies, NGOs, research institutes, citizens and students, you have the opportunity to develop innovative solutions for Wuppertal´s climate challenges. The event offers a varied programme consisting of expert contributions, workshops and especially time for ideation and business model development. During the 24 hours, the teams will be assisted by professional coaches and experts. The best ideas – awarded by a jury – will be subsequently promoted and further developed. The event takes place mainly in German.

+++ To provide you professional support as well as good food, we ask for a participation fee of 20 €. For that, after the registration on this website, you need to buy a Climathon Wuppertal ticket at https://climathon-wuppertal.eventbrite.de +++

Frequently asked questions

What does the participation in the Climathon Wuppertal cost?

Participation in the Climathon Wuppertal costs 20 €. After you have registered here on the Climathon website, buy a ticket at http://climathon-wuppertal.eventbrite.de to complete the registration. If you need financial support please contact us.

When does the Climathon Wuppertal start and end?

The Climathon Wuppertal starts on october 26 2018 at 9:00 a.m. and ends on october 27 2018 at 1:30 p.m., including the 24-hour main working time. Detailed information regarding the schedule can be found at https://climathon.climate-kic.org/wuppertal#theevent (scroll down a bit).

Do the participants work 24 hours non-stop?

No, not necessarily. It´s up to you to take a break and get some sleep during the night. Simple sleeping places are available on site. You can also sleep at home or in a hotel and come back the next morning to continue. The program on the 26th will last until midnight.

I have chosen a challenge when registering, but would like to work on another one. Can I change it?

Yes. You can change your choice at the beginning of the event.

Do I need to have an IT or technical background in order to participate in the Climathon Wuppertal?

No. Anyone who wants to work on innovative solutions to climate challenges can participate. The more diverse a group of participants is, the better the solution can be!

What do I need to bring to the Climathon Wuppertal?

Bring your laptop, mobile phone, etc. with you and think of all necessary power supplies and charging cables. Since the Climathon will last over 24 hours, you should of course also bring the necessary hygiene products, utensils for sleeping (e.g. sleeping mat and sleeping bag) and comfortable clothing. Catering will be provided on site. And of course bring a lot of motivation and ideas!

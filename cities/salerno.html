Together we can make Salerno and the world a better place to live!

Improving the productivity of urban and neighboring rural areas, particularly in the northern area of the city; fostering sustainable and inclusive development models based on smart agriculture. A stimulating opportunity for authorities, citizens, entrepreneurs: accept the CHALLENGE!

Salerno is a town of 134 057 inhabitants, the second largest municipality in Campania by population and 29th nationally.

Salerno was home to the Salernitan Medical School, which was the first and most important medical institution in Europe at the beginning of the Middle Ages. Since 1968 the city has been home to the University of Salerno, which has been located since 1988, in the form of a campus, in the neighboring municipalities of Fisciano and Baronissi.

In recent years, it has been one of the first tourist destinations in the world due to its geographical location, which places it at the center between the Amalfi and the Cilento coast.
For further informations http://www.comune.salerno.it/

Schedule of the day

Check out our schedule for the 24-hours

At the University of Salerno Friday, October 12 there is the weighing of the project to the press

Presentation to the media

Organizers and partners of the marathon, will meet with the press, students and entrepreneurs to present the project and its objectives.

The marathon starts at 3 pm on October 25 at the University of Salerno, in the pharmacy department

Marathon, first day

On the first day of the marathon, teams are formed between the participants, the tender procedure and the evaluation criteria are explained. The competition begins.

The marathon ends at 3pm on October 26th

Marathon, final day

On the second and last day of the marathon the teams, helped by the tutors, prepare the presentation of the ideas which is held at 1 pm; At 2.30 pm the jury decrees the winner

Prizes

The winners of the marathon will have the opportunity to participate, free of charge, for the final stage of the Best Practice Award, a national event, where banks and financial intermediaries specialized in business creation and development seek out ideas to be funded

The Best Practice award is a national event organized by the Salerno industrial association. It is an event that every year brings together aspiring entrepreneurs from all over Italy as well as banks and investors from all over the world. The purpose of the event is to foster networking among entrepreneurs and, above all, to bring together venture capitalists with start-ups and entrepreneurs looking for partners.

Join the 24h brainstorm for Helsinki sustainable food revolution!

Help the city in creating sustainable urban food systems in the pilot area and in developing smart local economy in food retail and consumption.

Helsinki’s vision is to be the world’s most functional city. In pursuing this vision, it seeks to create the best conditions possible for urban life for its residents and for visitors. Functionality is based on equality, non-discrimination, strong social cohesion and open, inclusive ways of operating. Helsinki also takes its own responsibility for the prevention of climate change seriously and ambitiously. Helsinki sets the goal of reducing emissions by 60 per cent by 2030, and brings forward its target of carbon neutrality to 2035 instead of 2050, as earlier. There is a potential to make an impact by creating new innovations for urban food systems including solutions for production, consumption, retail, waste management and energy. Tukkutori/Teurastamo district is a test bed for new food related soltions - we are waiting for your ideas!

What's in it for me?

  • Me
  • My city
  • The world

By joining the Climathon you and your team will get a chance to cultivate your ideas of sustainable Helsinki into concepts that can be used in the city deveopment. 

The City of Helsinki has a set a target to become a carbon neutral city by the end of 2035. There is a great potential to make an impact by creating new innovations for urban food systems including solutions for production, consumption, retail, waste management and energy. The city want's to develop the Tukkutori/Teurastamo district into a pilot area for sustainable urban food systems and local economy. Helsinki climate action can also contribute to the creation of new jobs and sustainable businesses.

By joining the Climathon you will help in solving the toughest climate challenges cities face today!

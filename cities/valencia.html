We want, we think, we create a better València

Climathon València faces the challenge of fighting the tiger mosquito in our city. Its presence is the indisputable consequence of climate change

Join the Climathon in València. Participate in this event where you can contribute, from different disciplines and with a transversal vision, in innovative solutions for the fight against the tiger mosquito. Share your ideas and connect with other teams. We will work for 24 hours with the support of mentoring from applied and academic research and from different profiles ranging from biology or health to strategic innovation.

Join Climathon in Valencia

Fight against the tiger mosquito in the city of València

The presence of this invasive species is a direct consequence of climate change. In recent years it has been spreading throughout the Mediterranean basin due to the increase in temperatures. We want to define, with a transversal focus, possible solutions and innovative elements to face it.

How can we fight the tiger mosquito in an eco-friendly way?

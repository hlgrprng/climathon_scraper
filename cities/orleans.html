Together we can make Orleans Métropole and the world a better place to live!

Get into the challenge to create new ways of flood risk management in order to improve our resilience process. Be an actor for solving climate challenge.

Orléans Metropole is crossed by the Loire, the last savage river in Europe. Even if no major event occured in 20th century, territories led studies in order to describe and understand the risk, wrote plans and built equipment to protect population. But climate change will increase our vulnerability. We decided to write a global plan to enhance our resilience capacities but also to decrease our carbon impact.

But there are still so many questions before becoming resilient...

Help us to write a new vision. Experts and mentors will be there to support your team in order to find better solutions, all together. If you are open minded, ready to work with various people: join us!

What's in it for me?

  • Me
  • My city
  • The world

Come and meet our experts, our activity coaches and during 24 hours imagine a new way of dealing with climate change.

Discover a fantastic place : 14 000 sq.m dedicated to creativity and innovation few minuts ago to our historical center

We know that despite all the studies we have produced, all the equipments we have already build, our territory is steal threatened by a major risk. Climate change will increase the frequency and the intensity of this phenomena. So we need to invent new ways of dealing with this risk. New brains coming from various origins should be able, all together, to be part of that new way of thinking.

With climate change, flood risk will be a common risk in many territories. The solutions that would be product during the Cimathon will be shared with all the local authorities, all the citizens, ... in fact with all that will feel concerned.

Don't forget that flood is killing thousands people through the world each year, with huge damage costs. We need to act !

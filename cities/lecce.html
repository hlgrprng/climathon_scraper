Together we can make Lecce and the world a better place to live!

Create a team and develop solutions for climate challenge put forward by the City of Lecce

Joining the Lecce Climathon is getting in touch with city officials and partners to bring about a shared vision for a more sustainable city. It's about turning that vision into a reality by working together with architects, agronomists and scientists.

Come with an existing idea or simply work on a new solution with an inspiring team. Experts and mentors will support your team during the 24 hours in finding the best solutions to the challenges.

All the ideas will be submitted to a jury of experts with the task of selecting the best project:

  • Rita Miglietta - Municipality of Lecce
  • Laura Panzera - Executive Director
  • Paola Mercogliano - Division Director
  • Domenico Camarda - Polytechnic University of Bari
  • Francesca Felline - Engineers' Association of Lecce
  • Sergio Ventura -  Architect
  • Rocco De Matteis - President of Architects' Association of Lecce
  • Riccardo Buccolieri - University of Salento

  • Me

Are you an ideas person? Do you have a passion for the green spaces and the interest for the climate changes? Do you like to learn new things and meeting interesting people? Do you like food, architecture or to stay around people?
